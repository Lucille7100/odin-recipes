Buko Pandan





Description

A famous Filipino cold dessert. You can never go wrong with this one. Made from fresh coconut and Pandan flavoured jelly that is very easy to make. ALL HAIL BUKO PANDAN!


Ingredients

  • 2 packs- Unlfavored jelly powder (green or buko pandan flavor)
  • 1/2 cup- Sugar
  • Pandan leaves or Buko pandan extract
  • 500ml- All purpose cream
  • 150ml- Condense milk
  • 2 cups, shredded- Fresh coconut
  • 1/2 cup, diced- Cheese
  • P A T I E N C E, again and again

Steps

  1. Cook gelatin as per instructions
  2. Add pandan leaves or buko pandan extract and 1/2 cup sugar into boiling gelatin mix.
  3. Stir thoroughly
  4. When done, transfer in a container and let it cool to set.
  5. Sliced jelly into cubes and set aside.
  6. In your food bowl, mix the all purpose cream and condense milk.
  7. Add to the mixture your jelly, cheese and shredded coconut.
  8. Mix everything gently.
  9. Let's put your patience to the test this time, put the mixture in the fridge to cool for around 2-3 hours.
  10. Lastly, dig in!
