Adobo

A Filipino adobo meal is a savory and tangy dish of meat, seafood, or vegetables, typically chicken or pork, braised in a mixture of vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, bay leaves, and black peppercorns, resulting in tender meat and a rich, flavorful sauce.

Sinigang

Sinigang is a tamarind-based soup that is known for its sour and savory taste. You can make it with either fish, pork belly, spare ribs or corned beef.

Buko Pandan

Buko Pandan is a popular Filipino cold dessert that features young coconut (buko), pandan-flavored jelly, and a creamy mixture of condensed milk and cream, creating a sweet, refreshing, and aromatic dessert that is a staple at Filipino parties and celebrations.
